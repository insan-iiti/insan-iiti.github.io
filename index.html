Welcome to the Intelligent Networking, Services, and Applications (INSAN) Research Group at IIT Indore. The research group is lead by Dr. Ayan Mondal and primarily focuses on research in intelligent systems enabling network technologies, service, and applications including Internet of Things (IoT), Edge Cloud, Software-Defined Networks (SDNs), Sensor-Cloud, Edge/Fog Networks, Wireless Ad-hoc and Sensor Networks, and Game-Theoretic Applications. If you are interested to be part of the INSAN research group and motivated to contribute, please email your CV to ayanm [AT] iiti [DOT] ac [DOT] in with a detailed research proposal.
